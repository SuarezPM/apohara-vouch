THEMIS

The scales for AP invoice audit.

One invoice → five-agent debate → Ed25519-signed Evidence Packet. Built for buyers-side fraud detection: phantom vendors, math fraud, duplicate submissions, price gouges. EU AI Act Art. 12 record-keeping by default, with offline-verifiable BLAKE3 + Ed25519 anchors.

Every Evidence Packet populates the booleans for these four frameworks. No opt-in. No add-on tier. The audit log is the product.
